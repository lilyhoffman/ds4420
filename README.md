# Hot Hand Analysis in Basketball

**Overview:** Investigates the “hot hand” which is whether players are more likely to make a shot after consecutive makes. Early research suggested a fallacy, but recent Bayesian and time-series studies show measurable hot/cold states.

**Methods:**  
- Time-series analysis of sequential shot data  
- Bayesian modeling of latent hot/cold states  
- NBA game, free throw, and experimental data

**Goal:** Quantify streaks, compare models, and inform strategy.
